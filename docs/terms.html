Factors Influencing Measurements

Many factors will influence the latency measurements observed by this service. Other users downloading large files or streaming video, for example, will cause a connection to seem slower than what may be possible if other activity wasn't present. The geographical distance from your computer to the web server may also play a factor. Overall, you shouldn't take the numbers too seriously or use them for anything important.
